The house

A modern press with an old-world respect for the book.

Orquideum is an independent publishing house devoted to intelligent, emotionally resonant nonfiction.

We publish compact, high-impact books that meet readers at the hinge points of modern life: changing careers, understanding money, protecting energy, rebuilding confidence, and becoming someone new without losing what matters.

Our list is intentionally focused, but never narrow. We are drawn to work that is useful, beautifully expressed, and culturally aware, whether it speaks to a specific season of life or a much broader human question.

“We believe the best nonfiction does more than inform. It gives language to what a reader has been carrying, then offers a way forward.”

Orquideum editorial principle
